**User Impact:** Cannot print - core function broken

**What Users Say:**
> "WiFi connection error every month two weeks out of the month. I have to have the printer turned off sometimes for a week straight to get it to reset."

> "Printer hardly ever connects correctly, have to reboot it almost every day for it to connect to WiFi."

> "Loses wifi connection every day. Disappointed."

- Connection drops requiring daily reboots
- App cannot find printer on same network

## SENIOR PM ANALYSIS: MOVING THE NEEDLE

### The Real Problem

HP has a **product-market fit crisis disguised as an app problem**.

Users don't hate printing - they hate feeling controlled. The subscription model has shifted HP from "trusted tool" to "predatory gatekeeper" in user perception.

### What Will Actually Move the Needle

| Priority | Action | Why It Matters | Success Metric |
|----------|--------|----------------|----------------|
| **#1** | Fix WiFi/Connectivity | 15% of ALL reviews. Every failed print is brand erosion. | 15% → 5% complaint rate |
| **#2** | Simplify Setup | First impression is permanently damaged. | 30+ min → <10 min |
| **#3** | Fix Subscription Perception | These users become anti-HP advocates. | <1% "scam" mentions |

### What NOT to Do

1. **Don't add more features** - fix the basics first
2. **Don't hide behind "user error"** - the app needs to be smarter
3. **Don't send more marketing emails** - users already feel overwhelmed
4. **Don't require more permissions** - reduce friction, don't add it

### 30-Day Action Plan

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| Week 1 | Ship quick-reconnect button and connection status indicator |
| Week 2 | Remove credit card requirement, make account optional |
| Week 3 | Reduce setup to 5 steps with clear progress bar |
| Week 4 | Launch in-app network diagnostic tool |
